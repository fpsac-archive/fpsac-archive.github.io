* Previous FPSAC conferences
- Definitions
  - *Article* is a preprint of a talk or a poster. An article may not be identical with its published version in proceedings. (An article, or any version of it, may not even appear in  proceedings.)
  - *Proceedings* is a collection of articles published in a journal or as a book.
  - *Extended abstract* is an article (of at most 12 pages) of a talk or
    a poster in FPSAC conferences after 2007.
  - *Local site* is a copy of a FPSAC website.
  - *Local proceedings* is a single pdf file containing all articles in a FPSAC conference.
- Notes
  - Every FPSAC conference until 2002 has proceedings, which contain selected papers presented at the conference (possibly accompanied by some original papers).
  - Since 2008, the extended abstracts of all talks and posters presented at every FPSAC conference have been published in a special issue of DMTCS or SLC.
