* Previous FPSAC conferences
- Every FPSAC conference until 2002 has proceedings published in a journal or as a book, which contain selected papers presented at the conference possibly accompanied by some original papers.
- Since 2008, the extended abstracts of all talks and posters presented at every FPSAC conference have been published in a special issue of DMTCS or SLC.
