Papers and Posters

Authors are invited to submit extended abstracts of at most twelve pages by November 15, 1999

Prefered way of submission is using the submission servor of the conference available through the Internet at the http address

http://www.liafa.jussieu.fr/~fpsac00

If an author is not able to use the submission servor, he can either send one single postcript file at the address

fpsac00@liafa.jussieu.fr

or send four copies of the extended abstract to the following address:

LIAFA - Universite Paris 7 - Case 7014

fpsac00@cnit.msu.ru

or by sending four copies of their extended abstract at the following address :

Moscow State University
Department of Mechanics and Mathematics
At the attention of Prof. A.V. Mikhalev - FPSAC'00

The submitted papers should begin with a summary written in English and in another official language of the conference (translations will be provided if necessary). Authors should indicate the mode of presentation which they consider appropriate for their paper, i.e. lecture or poster session. The notifications of acceptance or rejection are scheduled for January 15, 2000.


Open problem session

Contributions to the problem session are invited in advance of the conference dates. People interested in submitting a problem should submit it as described above, before June 1, 2000.


Software demonstrations

Demonstrations of software relevant to the topics of the conference are encouraged. People interested in giving a software demonstration should submit a paper including the hardware requirements, as describeb above, before January 15, 2000.
