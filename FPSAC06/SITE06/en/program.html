Welcome Reception and Conference Banquet

The welcome reception is to be held at 17:30 on Sunday, June 18, 2006. The location is along Mission Bay a short walk south from the hotel between San Jose Pl and Santa Clara Pl at Mission Bay. Parking is available at the corner of Santa Clara Pl and Mission Boulavard.

The conference banquet is to be held on Thursday, June 22, 2006, along Mission Bay adjacent to the hotel.
