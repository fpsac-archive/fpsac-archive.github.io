Registration Details

Registration fees include attendance at all sessions, the opening reception, and coffee breaks. Meals, however, are not included in the registration fee.

The cost of attending the conference banquet is US$70 per person, which can be paid at the time of registration.

All participants are encouraged to register by the Early Registration Deadline of April 1, 2006.

Registration opens on March 1, 2006 and will remain open through the beginning of the conference.

Conference Fees

On or Before April 1st, 2006:
Standard Registration Fee: US$250
Student Registration Fee: US$125

After April 1st, 2006:
Standard Registration Fee: US$325
Student Registration Fee: US$170
