Registration

Registration fees include attendance at all sessions, the opening reception, and coffee breaks. Meals, however, are not included in the registration fee.

Registration fee
on or before April 30, 2012 after April 30, 2012
Regular 20,000 JPY 30,000 JPY
Student* 10,000 JPY 15,000 JPY

*with verification, such as a copy of student ID or a letter from the advisor.

Banquet (August 2) 6,500 JPY (per person)
Excursion (August 1) 4,500 JPY (per person)

Note that these fees are in Japanese Yen.

Participants are requested to register online through the following site at Kinki Nippon Tourist:

Registration site (Kinki Nippon Tourist)
https://biz.knt.co.jp/ecs2.5

On this system, you can also reserve a hotel room and register for the banquet and the excursion. All participants are encouraged to register by the Early Registration Deadline of April 30, 2012, although the registration will remain open until July 15, 2012.

The registration site consists of the following steps (in this order):

  1. Personal Data
  2. Registration
  3. Banquet
  4. Excursion
  5. Hotel Accommodations
  6. Method of Payment; by credit card (VISA, Master Card, American Express, Diners Club or JCB only)

Cancellation Policy

Please note that an administrative charge of 1,000 JPY will apply to all cancellations made on or before June 30, 2012. No refunds will be given for cancellations made after this date. Cancellation requests should be sent by email to fpsac12 [at sign] math.nagoya-u.ac.jp
