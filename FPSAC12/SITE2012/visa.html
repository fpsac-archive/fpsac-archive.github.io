Visa Information

Nationals of these countries and regions

List of Countries and Regions That Have Visa Exemption Arrangements with Japan
(Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Japan)
http://www.mofa.go.jp/j_info/visit/visa/short/novisa.html#list

are not required to obtain a visa to enter Japan when the purpose of their visit is commerce, a conference, tourism, visit to relatives/acquaintances, etc.

Nationals of countries that are not on the above list need a visa to attend FPSAC’12. (e.g. China, Russia or NIS countries, Philippines, India.)

Information about applying for a visa can be found here:

Guide to Japanese Visas (Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Japan)
http://www.mofa.go.jp/j_info/visit/visa/index.html

People having inquiries about visa should call the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Japan at

phone +81 3-5501-8431.

If you apply a visa, you will need a letter of invitation from the organizing committee. Please contact the organizing committee at

email fpsac12 [at sign] math.nagoya-u.ac.jp.
