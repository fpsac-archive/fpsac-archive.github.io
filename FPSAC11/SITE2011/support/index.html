Participant Support

We expect to provide limited financial support to contribute to travel and living expenses during the conference. First priority will be given to graduate students, postdocs and junior faculty, and--in exceptional cases--to qualified undergraduates.

To request support, send an email and also ask a mentor to write a supporting letter. Both should be sent to Sergey Kitaev at

fpsac2011+support@gmail.com.

If you are a student then this supporting letter should preferably be written by your faculty supervisor.

Requests for support should be emailed to the address above no later than March 1, 2011, and contain the following (text only, no attachments, please):

  • E-mail Address
  • University/Organization
  • Current Position

  • University at which you did your PhD
