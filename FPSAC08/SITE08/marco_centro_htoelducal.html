This very nice hotel, next to the sea, is within a 5 minute walking distance from the Sheraton Miramar Hotel & Convention Center. The special conference rates are CLP$51.000 (CLP$60.690 with IVA) for the basic room (single or double occupancy), CLP$59.500 (CLP$70.805 with IVA) for the junior suite (with a jacuzzi and a small extra room), and CLP$76.571 (CLP$91.120 with IVA) for the full suite (with jacuzzi and two separate rooms). To make a reservation at these special rates, send an e-mail to capducalv@mi.cl (mentionning that you are a participant in the Mathematics Conference organized by the University of Talca) with the following information:

Name
Date and approximate time of arrival at the hotel
Date of departure
Type of room
Credit card number with expiration date
Phone number
Number of people that will share the room
