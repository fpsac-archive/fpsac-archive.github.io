This hotel is located within a 12 minute walking distance from the Sheraton Miramar Hotel & Convention Center. It is also very close to the Casino of Viña del Mar and many fine restaurants.

Hotel Monterilla offers a 20% discount to the FPSAC participants (you need to mention in your e-mail that you are a participant in the Mathematics Conference organized by the University of Talca). When prices are listed in Chilean pesos and in dollars such as here, they sometimes do not correspond to the actual exchange rate. When checking out, you should obviously ask to pay in pesos if it is cheaper than in dollars and vice-versa.
