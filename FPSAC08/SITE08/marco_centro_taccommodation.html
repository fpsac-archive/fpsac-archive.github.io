Automatized reservation system

To access the online reservation system please click here and follow the instructions (for English, choose Idioma: English in the northeast corner of the page). Please note that reservations must be made before the 11th of June (that is, untill the 10th of June).

To cancel your reservation, enter the online reservation system again. Click on Check Reservations. Enter your reservation number and proceed to cancellation. Note that reservations can be cancelled without penalty before the 23rd of May (that is, until de 22nd of May). Cancellations made between the 23rd of May and the 6th of June will be subject to a penalty corresponding to the price of the first night of the reservation. Cancellations made after the 6th of June (that is, starting the 7th of June) are not refundable.
