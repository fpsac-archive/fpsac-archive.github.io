To make a reservation at these special rates, send an e-mail to venta.habitacion@marinadelrey.cl (mentionning that you are a participant in the Mathematics Conference organized by the University of Talca) with the following information:

Name
Date and approximate time of arrival at the hotel
Date of departure
Type of room
Credit card number with expiration date

Please note that to obtain this special rate, you need to pay in chilean pesos (which you are entitled to do). Therefore, do not accept to pay in dollars unless you are sure that it basically corresponds to the price in chilean pesos.
