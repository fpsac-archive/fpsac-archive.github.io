White paper of FPSAC'03

This is the white paper of the FPSAC'03. The aim of the paper is to highlight
some of the recent progress, capture some of the most important current directions,
and try to delineate some of the emerging trends in algebraic combinatorics based on
discussions at FPSAC'03.
