FPSAC 15th Anniversary
International Conference on
Formal Power Series and Algebraic Combinatorics
FPSAC'03

June 23 -- 27, 2003

Linköping University (Sweden)

Third announcement -- Registration opens


Topics

All aspects of combinatorics and their relations with other parts of mathematics, physics, computer science and biology.

Conference program

To help us celebrate the 15th anniversary of FPSAC, Alain Lascoux and Richard Stanley will be speaking on their perspectives on algebraic combinatorics. We will also have a special session on combinatorial problems in biology.

Participant support

Funds are available for partial support of participants, in particular for students and junior scientists who are studying or working in the U.S. or who are U.S. citizens or permanent residents. Requests should contain a letter of recommendation and include the estimated transportation and living expenses for the duration of the conference as well as the amount of any support available from other sources. All requests should be sent by February 28, 2003 by email to fpsac@mai.liu.se.
