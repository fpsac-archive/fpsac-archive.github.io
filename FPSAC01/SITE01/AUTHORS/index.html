Instructions for the authors


Papers and Posters

Your camera-ready manuscript is due on February 26, 2001. The final copy is limited to 10 pages generated by AMS-LaTeX with package amsart. Don't use A4 format ! Use US letter format.

If possible, please use our example file as a template, at least the first six lines, which give the page size and margins.

Please send your final file to the submission account

fpsac01s@math.la.asu.edu

Please send two mails :
  • one mail with subject "tex file" followed by the FPSAC number of your paper, attached your tex file.
  • one mail with subject "postscript file" followed by the FPSAC number of your paper, attached your postscript file.
Here below are example files:
  • example.tex
  • example.ps
If you do not have AMS-LaTeX on your system, you may download it from the AMS.

SUMMARY


The papers should contain a summary in English and in one of the other official languages of the conference (translations will be provided if necessary).

FIGURES


If your paper includes some figures then send one mail for each figure. Each mail will have subject : "name-figure" followed by the FPSAC number of your paper. "name-figure" must be the name of your figure in your tex file.

BIBLIOGRAPHY


If you use a \bibliography command with a bibtex file, then mail it with subject "name-bibtex" followed by the FPSAC number of your paper. "name-bibtex" must be the name of your bibliography file in your tex file.
