Topics :
Algebraic and bijective combinatorics and their relations with other parts of mathematics, combinatorial and computer algebra, computer science and physics.
Conference Program :
  • Invited lectures
  • Contributed presentations and poster session
  • Problem session and software demonstrations

A special session will be dedicated to Rodica Simion works. Co-authors of Rodica Simion are encouraged to send a poster presentation of their joint work.

Official Languages :
