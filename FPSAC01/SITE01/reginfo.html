In order to attend FPSAC'01. you need to do the following:

  • Reserve your hotel room directly with the hotel or arrange to share a room.
  • Send your registration fee to Arizona State University as soon as possible.
  • Fill out the online registration form as soon as possible.

Hotel

The conference will be held at the Holiday Inn, Old Town Scottsdale. We have reserved a block of rooms at this hotel, and strongly recommend that visitors stay there. Old Town Scottsdale is a cultural and commercial center, with many restaurants, shops, and galleries and the Scottsdale Center for the Arts within walking distance.

Registration fee

The early registration deadline has passed. Registration is $300 US, with a reduced rate of $150 US for students. Please register in advance if possible; this helps in planning, and ensures that you will be reserved a copy of the conference proceedings.

US residents, please send your registration fee by check or money order, payable to Arizona State University.

The closest airport is Phoenix Sky Harbor Airport, approximately 10 miles from Scottsdale. It is served by all major US airlines.
