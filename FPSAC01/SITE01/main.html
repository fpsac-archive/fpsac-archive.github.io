Topics :
Algebraic and bijective combinatorics and its relation with other parts of mathematics, combinatorial and computer algebra, computer science and physics.

Invited lectures, contributed presentations, poster sessions, problem sessions and software demonstrations. A special session will be dedicated to the work of Rodica Simion. Co-authors of Rodica Simion are encouraged to send a poster presentation of their joint work.
Official Languages :
The official languages of the conference are English, French and Spanish.
