The Mathematical Legacy of Rodica Simion

Richard Stanley

To appear at Formal Power Series and Algebraic Combinatorics (FPSAC01), Tempe, Arizona (USA), May 20-24, 2001

Abstract

Algebraic combinatorics lost one of its most enthusiastic adherents with the tragic death of Rodica Simion on January 7, 2000. We will give a survey of her work and its subsequent influence on combinatorics. Topics will include polynomials with real zeros, permutation enumeration, noncrossing partitions, and shuffle posets.
