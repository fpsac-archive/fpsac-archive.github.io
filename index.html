2. Previous FPSAC conferences

  • Definitions
    • Conference proceedings is a collection of papers published in a journal or as a book.
    • Article is a preprint of a talk or a poster. An article may not be identical with its published version in conference proceedings. (An article, or any version of it, may not even appear in conference proceedings.)
    • Extended abstract is an article (of at most 12 pages) of a talk or a poster in FPSAC conferences after 2007.
    • Local site is a copy of a FPSAC website.
  • Notes
    • Every FPSAC conference until 2002 has conference proceedings, which contain selected papers presented at the conference (possibly accompanied by some original papers).
    • Since 2008, the extended abstracts of all talks and posters presented at every FPSAC conference have been published in a special issue of DMTCS or SLC.
