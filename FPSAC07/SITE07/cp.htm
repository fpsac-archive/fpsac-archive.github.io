Registration:
Registration desks are set both at the lobby of Jia Yuan hotel and the Huibin Garden Hotel on Sunday, July 1, 2007 from 8:00am---8:00pm. You can pick up the conference package upon check in.
From July 2, the registration desk will be set at the conference site, Shiing-Shen Hall .
Welcome Reception:

The welcome reception is to be held at 18:30 on Sunday, July 1, 2007.

Location: Mingzhu Graden Hotel (明珠园) on campus (see the map).

For all the participants lived in Huibin Garden hotel, there will be a shuttle outside of the Huibin Garden Hotel at 17:30.

Talks and Posters:
Poster presentations will have access to one side of a poster-board of size width 0.7m, height 1.2m (about 12 A4 papers). Poster sections are in the afternoon of Tuesday, July 2 and Thursday, July 4. Please check the program online to see the list of posters on each day.
Each contributed talk lasts 30 minutes including 5 minutes for questions. Both overhead projector and computer projector are available. The overhead projector can work with either transparencies or papers
Meals:

The registration fee covers the buffet lunches and suppers during the conference. Beer is available at the buffet suppers.

Location: Mingzhu Graden Hotel (明珠园) on campus (see the map).

There is also a cash-bar for wines.

 
Every participant needs to enter to the dinning hall with your meal ticket. Non-registered accompany person can buy the tickets from the registration desk. The rate is
Lunch: ￥37.5 ($5) per person for one day
Supper: ￥37.5 ($5) per person for one day
Chinese Acrobatic and Music Show:

There is a Chinese acrobatic and music show on Tuesday, July 3, 2007.

Location: is Tianjin Theatre (天津礼堂).

Address: No 24, Youyi Road, Hexi District, Tianjin (天津市河西区友谊路24号) .

 
We will provide transportation at the lobby of Yi Yuan Hotel at 18:30. Every participant needs to enter the arena with the show ticket. The number on your ticket is the bus number you should get in. Non-registered accompany person can buy the show ticket from the registration desk. The rate is ￥104 ($15 ) per person.
Banquet:

The conference banquet will be held on Thursday, July 5, 2007, at the following restaurant.

Location: Pengtiange Restaurant (鹏天阁酒楼)

Address: No 8, Hongqi Road, Nankai District, Tianjin. (南开区红旗南路8号) Tel: 23958888.

Alcoholic drinks are available at the banquet.

 

We will provide transportation at the lobby of Shiing-shen Building (see map) at 17:30. Every participant needs to enter the arena with the banquet ticket. The number on your ticket is the bus number you should get in. Non-registered accompany person can buy the ticket from the registration desk. The rate is ￥104 ( $15 ) per person.

Conference Site:
The conference will be held at the Shiing-shen Building (省身楼) of Nankai University, where the Chern Institute of Mathematics is located, which is named after the late Professor S. S. Chern.

The Shiing-shen Building is located in the central teaching zone of Nankai University . On its east is a lawn, where the statue of Premier Zhou Enlai stands in the very centre. Inside the Shiing-Shen Building are the library, lecture halls, and more than 60 offices.
