Taxi on Campus
 
Taxi can be easily found in front of Yiyuan Hotel (see map). It can also be found at Sounthwest Gate (see map). The rate for taxis is RMB 8 for the first 3km , then RMB 1.7/km or RMB 2.0/km.
Currency Exchange
 
The name of Chinese Currency is RenMinBi (RMB). At the rate of June 25, 2007, 1USD=7.71RMB. Currency Exchange stations are available at the airport and many local banks. On July 2, Bank Of China will have a counter at the registration desk (Secretary Room 107 in Shiing-shen Building) at 12:00pm and can exchange currency for conference participants. They can accept travel cheque and US dollars.
Credit Cards are accepted in many major hotels, but not by a typical local business. Automatic Teller Machines (ATM) are available at each bank. There are two on campus. You can only withdraw RMB from the local ATM.
