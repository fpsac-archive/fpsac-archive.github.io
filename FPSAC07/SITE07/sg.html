Trading Districts    
 
The larger trading districts and streets are Binjiang Road , Heping Road , Xiaobai Building, Clothing Street , Trading City in the southwest corner, Zhongshan Road . Tonglou and Nanlu. The busiest are Binjiang Road Trading Street. cm the west haul, of Haihe River , and Heping Road Trading Street. The shops on the two streets include old places such as Quanyechang, Hua- Trade Mansion , Department Store, Tianjin Shoe Store, Hengdeli Clock and Wristwatch Store, Guanshengyuan Food Store and Sheng- Hat Store. The streets also have shopping centers and authorized sellers such as Binjiang Trade Mansion , Gilly Building , and International Emporium. Walking St. on the Binjiang Rd. features variety stores and attracts many tourists. In the evening people eat snacks beneath the colored lamps in front of these stores. If you will take a taxi to get there from Nankai University , it will cost around 20RMB.

Ancient Culture Street    
 
Ancient Culture Street has more than a hundred small shops which sell old books, antiques, jade, traditional handicraft articles, folk craft, Yang Liuqing folk paintings, Zhang color clay figures, Wei's kites, Liu's brick carvings, Peking enamel, double-sided embroidery, paintings and many other articles. Taxi fare from Nankai University to the Ancient Culture Street costs around 20RMB.
Here is a card you can show to the driver. (print version).

Food Street    
 
Food Street has many famous restaurants with many dishes from all areas in China. There are menus from the famous imperial menus to the simple meals of country flavor. You can also find some restaurants that serve western dinners. At present, it is the largest business group selling Chinese food. If you will take a taxi to get there from Nankai University , it will cost around 30RMB.
