Travel in Tianjin

The Tianjin Shi Mansion:

   
The Shi Mansion , located in the 1,000 year-old town of Yang Liuqing , was the old residence of the Shi family, one of the eight prominent families in Tianjin in the Qing Dynasty, and was called the “No.1 Mansion in North China .”
It covers an area of 6,080 square meters and has four rows of buildings of antique brick, wood and stone carvings. The mansion has been opened as Yang Liuqing Folk Customs Museum . There is a 300-meter-long street which covers an area of 20,000 square meters, on both sides of which stand houses in Ming and Qing styles, with a total floor space of 10,000 square meters.
It's opened at 9:00am and closed at 5:00pm. The price is 20RMB/person. Taxi fare from Nankai University to the Tianjin Shi Mansion costs around 150RMB.

Please take me to the Shi Mansion in the Yang Liuqing.
 
Zhou Enlai and Deng Yingchao Memorial Hall:    
 
Covering an area of 6,600 square meters, the memorial hall by the north side of Water Park was opened on March 5, 1998 to commemorate the centenary of the birth of the late Premier Zhou Enlai. Included in the building, there is an entrance-hall, exhibition rooms that illustrate Zhou's life with historic photographs, his correspondences and personal collection, a video show room, a multi-purpose hall, distinguished guests' lounge, a library of archives. Situated in the entrance-hall are the white jade sitting statues of Zhou and his wife, Deng Yingchao. Covering the walls are the bas-reliefs of landscapes and the revolutionary masses. Taxi fare from Nankai University to the memorial hall costs around 10RMB.

Water Park    
 
More than half of Water Park 's area is covered with water, which makes this park a lovely and special one. In the park there are twelve small islands connected by arch bridges and small mounds. Weeping willows along the waters edge and lotus flowers in the water make this a relaxing place to visit. Taxi fare from Nankai University to the Water Park costs around 10RMB.
