dormitory roooms

Please note that the deadline for reserving a dorm room has passed. At this point we cannot accept any more requests for dorm accommodations.

The rooms are $36 per person per night and are only available for the full period (double occupancy, including breakfast) with an arrival date of Sunday, August 1, and a departure day of Saturday August 7, 2010. Guest accommodations will be available at 4:00 PM on the day of arrival (although you can store your luggage at the dorms earlier if needed) and reserved until 11:00 AM on the day of departure. All dorm rooms come equipped with linens, towels, and soap.

hotels

The hotel options near San Francisco State University aren't great, but there are two hotels in walking distance from campus:

  • Days Inn San Francisco At The Beach (1.8 miles from San Francisco State University, one can take MUNI bus, calm area with same restaurants a 15 minute walk away). Please mention that you will be a participant at FPSAC and the hotel will give you rooms at a reduced rate (you can only get that by calling +1.415.665.9000).
  • Hampton Inn San Francisco-Daly City (very close to BART, 2.1 miles from San Francisco State University, one can take MUNI bus)

There are numerous hotels in downtown San Francisco, about a 20-minute streetcar ride away from San Francisco State University.

  • Chancellor Hotel will give you a discounted rate of $109 per room and night if you book before July 2--mention FPSAC when you make a reservation (downtown union square area, close to public transportation, take MUNI streetcar to San Francisco State University).
